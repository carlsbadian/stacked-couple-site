Targeted means exactly that: added for a specific, tracked outcome — not part of
the everyday floor. The daily foundation (protein, hydration, baseline
micronutrients) lives under [Fuel](/the-stacks/fuel/). This category is what we
layer on top, on purpose, with a metric attached.

## The standard every add-on has to meet

1. **A named goal.** Sleep depth, recovery, cognition, joint support — something specific.
2. **A number to move.** If we can't point at the marker it should shift, it doesn't go in.
3. **A review date.** Everything gets re-justified. No permanent passengers.

## What we publish here

- What we're running for which goal, and the reasoning.
- What the tracking said after a fair trial — including the ones that did nothing.
- What got cut, and why. The graveyard is as useful as the stack.
