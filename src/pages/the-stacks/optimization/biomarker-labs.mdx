Wearables read the outside. Labs read the inside. This category is the internal
data layer: blood work, diagnostics, and hormone panels — the part of the
experiment that keeps the rest honest.

## The testing frame

- **Baseline before anything.** No protocol starts without knowing the starting point.
- **Retest on a schedule.** Changes get verified, not assumed.
- **Data drives changes.** Protocol adjustments answer to the panel, not to how a week felt.

## What we publish here

- Which panels we run, how often, and what each one is actually for.
- How we prep for a draw so results are comparable across time.
- Reading your own results — reference ranges vs. optimal ranges, in plain language.
- The retest stories: what moved, what didn't, and what we changed because of it.
