The protocol is only as honest as the measurement behind it. This category is
the hardware and software ecosystem we run — what we measure, what we ignore,
and how we read the numbers without letting the numbers run the show.

## The measurement stack

- **Wearables** — sleep staging, HRV, resting heart rate, and recovery trends (Oura ring, Apple Watch).
- **Smart scales and body scans** — weight is one data point; composition is the story.
- **Tracking apps** — where the logs live and how they talk to each other.

## What we publish here

- Device reviews after real use — months, not unboxings.
- How we set baselines and what counts as signal vs. noise.
- The weekly read: which numbers we check, which we deliberately don't.
- When the tracker and the body disagree — and which one wins.
