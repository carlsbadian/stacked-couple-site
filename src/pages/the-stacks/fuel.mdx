Fuel is framework over daily detail. We're not a cooking channel — we're two
people with different goals eating from the same playbook, and this pillar shows
how that actually works.

## What lives here

- **Quick meals and real food** — not a cooking class.
- **Protein and hydration as non-negotiables** — plus the macro targets that anchor everything.
- **His plate vs. her plate** — different goals, same framework.
- **Intermittent fasting windows** — educational, not prescriptive.
- **Paleo / Whole30 frameworks** — explained simply, without the dogma.
- **Foundational daily nutrition and baseline supplementation** — the everyday floor everything else builds on.
- **Travel nutrition** — keeping the framework intact away from your own kitchen.

## Where the line is

Baseline daily nutrition lives here. Goal-specific and performance supplements —
the things we add for a specific, tracked outcome — live under
[Optimization → Targeted Supplements](/the-stacks/optimization/targeted-supplements/).
That split is deliberate: the foundation is not the experiment.
